# Big Money Dozer

A fun and engaging coin pusher game where players drop coins from the top and watch them cascade down the machine, pushing other coins and collecting rewards!

## Overview

Big Money Dozer is a classic arcade-style coin pusher game that combines strategy, timing, and luck. Drop coins strategically to maximize your winnings as they bounce and roll down the machine, pushing prizes and accumulating your fortune.

## Features

- **Addictive Gameplay**: Drop coins and watch them cascade down the machine
- **Strategic Elements**: Position your coins carefully to maximize returns
- **Multiple Prize Types**: Collect different coins and special rewards
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Game mechanics intensify as you advance
- **Score Tracking**: Keep track of your best performances
- **Smooth Graphics**: Enjoy polished, smooth animations

## Gameplay

1. **Drop Coins**: Click or tap to drop coins from the top of the machine
2. **Watch & Strategize**: Coins cascade down, pushing other coins and objects
3. **Collect Rewards**: Gather falling coins and special power-ups
4. **Accumulate Wealth**: Build up your score and unlock new features
5. **Compete**: Beat high scores and challenge yourself
